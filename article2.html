SAFIR Global Summit

We have been in the midst of SAFIR's year-end event together with its strategic partners and with the leaders and representatives of our relationship marketing network. We are talking about the SAFIR Global Summit, an event in which, beyond the luxury and fun that is lived and throbbing at all times, the news of the future of the company and the network have been shared; as well as the introduction of new personalities and new milestones reached for the SAFIR global brand.


Why should you attend the next event?

The events of a company, especially those related to the end of the year, are of unparalleled importance for your development as a Promoter and the development of your network. It is not about attending for the image that is reflected of being there, nor for the publications that you can share on your social networks to promote your brand or your community, but a corporate event oriented to the network is much more than appearances and pretty lights: it is a space for reflection, analysis, comparison, growth, learning and understanding from first hand.

It is in an event of the characteristics of the SAFIR Global Summit that we find the news of what is to come, and also the explanations of why and for what of this news. The performance of the entire year is also analyzed in all important aspects of the brand, such as SAFIR's marketing, corporate structure, planning, alliances, as well as legal ones.

You will know that living an experience in the first person is not the same as seeing it on a screen or through messages, or being told about it in a meeting or webinar. The same happens with the flow of information, its quantity and detail, and the level of doubts that can be dispelled. If you are at the event, you have your doubts fresh because you are conceiving them at the same time as the rest of the participants, therefore you have the opportunity in situ to clear up your doubts and to debate with your colleagues within the event about the pros and cons of each news.

As if this were not enough, you have the opportunity to connect with leaders who, both because of their experience and their enormous dedication to their work; can train you and give you extremely valuable tips on how to take the information, what to expect, how to work with it, how to reproduce it, how to simplify it and what kind of doubts to expect so that you can be prepared for future clients or Promoters of your network.

In addition to these clear and fundamental advantages of the SAFIR Global Summit, you will have complete freedom to enjoy top-level experiences, in an ecosystem made for your comfort and your growth, because the growth of each individual in the network is the growth of SAFIR in its whole. Your presence here is as significant as that of any other person since you have the potential to be a factor of change as every person who has been, is, and will be.

What does SAFIR think of corporate events?

As Werner Kaiser(Regional Director and CSO of SAFIR) emphasizes at every opportunity, strategic management of a brand (and its consequent team) is carried out through a global and strategically defined vision. In the case of SAFIR, this vision translates into 3 macro concepts that at first glance may seem simple, but in their execution require a total predisposition to growth, adapting all the necessary areas to the changing tides of the market and the industry.

We are then talking about a definition of what SAFIR will be, what it is working to achieve, and the greater objective that prevails and governs all the actions that the network carries out: Real, Serious, and Huge.

This playing field, which is so clearly defined by these 3 concepts, was present at the SAFIR Global Summit Event and was manifested both in its organization and in the nature of the announcements made.

In a strategic and holistic vision, SAFIR has demonstrates in this event its commitment to be Real, Serious, and Huge, and it has done so by providing the network (through the present attendees) with key and fundamental information on SAFIR and its performance.

Real: To be real, you have to do what you plan. There must be fruits and results in projects and planning. There has to be a commitment to do first, and with enormous room for improvement in each step taken. This is what SAFIR transmits by giving entity to the brand and the company, by bringing together so many people under the same objective, by associating with level partners, and by placing an excellent product portfolio in the portfolio of each Promoter that joins the adventure.

SAFIR Global Summit has brought real updates about real projects and real products to be the opportunity we all expect.

Serious: Seriousness is not given by a lack of joy, much less a lack of enjoyment (since the SAFIR Global Summit has been also a space for pleasure and enjoyment of the fruits achieved) but by the clarity and transparency of the commitments. Making commitments and carrying them out is being real, but the way they are carried out is serious.

SAFIR has a commitment to its Promoters as well as its strategic Partners and its clients, to have an optimal level of seriousness regarding all the commitments obtained, always doing the best possible in each situation to ensure a positive result and a unique experience.

SAFIR Global Summit has brought commitment and structure to shape each project and objective, through the hand of advisors and active professionals in the fields of Marketing, Communication, and Legal Analysis.

Huge: No critical hit can be made small. The big challenges, the big dreams, and the big plans; require great resources and commitment as well as great planning. When we talk about "huge" we do not only refer to the physical quantity, of objects or people; but we also talk about the level of impact of our commitment and our actions. It is part of the fundamental philosophy of SAFIR, to achieve a huge impact on each person, in the life of each member of the SAFIR promotion network, as well as that of each client who chooses to trust us. We want to be huge because we have huge benefits to give to the world, and the event of the magnitude of the SAFIR Global Summit in Dubai has been our definition of this.

SAFIR Global Summit has brought a huge impact on the Promoters and the SAFIR ecosystem, via new products and projects, better strategies, and thorough analysis.
