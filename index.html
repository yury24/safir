Zeniq Smartchain

The ZENIQ Blockchain and the second layer, the ZENIQ SmartChain, are the technical foundation for numerous unique services and possibilities of the ZENIQ ecosystem.
SmartChain, as technical foundation, enables the creation of smart contracts, NFTs/tokenization projects, applications for decentralized finance (DeFi) & Defi 2.0, the world of Web 3.0/tools & infrastructure, B2B/B2C solutions on the ecosystem and much more. The technical possibilities offered by the smart chain are almost unlimited.

• It is environmentally friendly (low energy consumption) since it uses Proof of Stake instead of Proof of Work.
